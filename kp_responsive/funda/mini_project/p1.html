KR$NA --- Lyrical Rapper

Krishna --- Sellout --- Prozpekt

Krishna Kaul known by his stage name KR$NA is the greatest lyrical rapper of India. Krishna was on 4th Oct 1987 to a Kashmiri Pandit family in Delhi. He started rapping by the age of fourteen to blend with his school friends in South London. Early in his career in 2006 he recorded and released songs on MySpace page.

He released "Kaisa Mera Desh" in 2010 which become overnight trend on youtube and also the first Indian hip hop song to trend on Youtube. In 2013 he released "Vijay" in collaboration with international NGO Save The Children. His socio-political songs touched people and also his awareness of politics and society as whole which is quite reflected in his music way-ahead in his career.

The underground rapper switched from Prozpekt to his current stage name KR$NA and signed a record deal with Universal Music in 2013. Whereby he released his first album "Sellout" in 2014 all in english. He is also the first Indian hip hop artist to do a five-city album tour in India.

Road to the KR$NA the greatest rapper of India.

Over his journey from start in 2002 to 2016 there came many instances when he had to go back to his normal life and leave music and after 2-years of break in 2016 he made comeback with his Hindi release "Vyanjan" song. The song hit success and he started walking the road to become the greatest rapper of India and never look back.

He released "Dekh Kaun Aaya Wapas" in 2017 with allan of his comeback which was a great success. He also signed DNH Artists(now Kalamkaar) in 2017 and is now partner. He has released sevral hit tracks after his comeback. We have seen many dhh and international collaborations of Kr$na.

When it comes to writing lyrics, metaphors and references there is no match to Krishna's talent you dare beef with him and he will go Kamakazi on you. He is very generous and humble and talks less but his songs speak for him and fills his audience with motivation and liveliness.
